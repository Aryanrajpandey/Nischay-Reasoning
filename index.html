Intelligence layer

Most AI systems relay intelligence.
Nischay generates it.

Beneath every conversation is a reasoning layer that runs before the LLM responds.

🧩
Behavioral Clustering
Groups your profile against 847 anonymized similar students. Shows observed outcomes at 3, 5, and 10 years. Tells you which cluster you're in — and what that cluster's trajectory looks like.
📊
Career Recommendation Ranking
Scores each potential career path against your specific profile vector: marks, city, financial tier, learning style, risk tolerance, and long-term preference signals. Not a list. A ranked probability distribution.
📈
Salary Trajectory Regression
Builds a salary model for your specific combination of college tier + city + role + year of graduation. Shows median, low, and high trajectories with what determines each.
⚠️
Dropout & Regret Risk
Calculates the probability that you will regret this decision based on: intrinsic motivation signals, external pressure ratio, alignment between stated goal and revealed preference.
🔍
Contradiction Scoring
Continuously measures the distance between your stated goals and your revealed constraints. A high contradiction score is a flag — not a judgment.
🧠
Personality Modeling
Maps Big Five traits, cognitive style, risk tolerance, and motivation patterns from conversational signals. Recommendations adapt when your personality profile updates. No forms required.
🎯
Confidence Calibration
Every output carries a confidence level with a listed reason. Low confidence = missing variables, weak data, or conflicting signals. You always know why Nischay is or isn't certain.
📉
Longitudinal Behavioral Analysis
Tracks how your signals change across sessions. Confidence trends. Pressure trends. Decision consistency. Avoidance patterns. The data source for behavioral drift detection.

Multi-agent architecture

Nine agents. One meta-brain.
The first time they disagree, you benefit.

Agents run in parallel. They don't always agree. The Meta-Reasoning Layer resolves their conflicts — and shows you the disagreement.

01
Profile Agent
Builds your context before advising you. Personality signals, learning style, emotional state, constraints. Feeds the intelligence engine.
02
Research Agent
Retrieves real career data. NIRF rankings, salary surveys, placement records, market demand. Cites every source. Never invents statistics.
03
Devil's Advocate
Challenges every assumption. Especially the ones you're attached to. Probability estimates, not vague warnings.
04
Salary Agent
Salary ranges by college tier, city, role, year. Always explained. Always a distribution, not a number. Sources cited.
05
Skill Gap Agent
Specific gaps. Specific resources. Measurable timelines. "Improve communication" is not feedback. This agent gives you the actual gap.
06
Follow-Up Agent
Tracks what was said in prior sessions. Checks progress. Corrects itself when advice was wrong. References specific advice by session number.
07
Resume Agent
Recruiter-level line-by-line feedback. ATS gaps, metric flags, verb-level suggestions. Before/After/Why for every note.
08
Contradiction Agent
Detects when your stated goals conflict with revealed constraints. Names it. Asks which matters more. Does not resolve it for you.
09
Trajectory Agent
Simulates your future across selected career paths. Stress, income, satisfaction, burnout, autonomy — modeled over 10 years. Shows the distribution.
META
Meta-Reasoning Layer
Runs after all agents complete. Resolves disagreements. Aggregates confidence scores. Identifies where agents diverge significantly and surfaces that divergence as meaningful uncertainty.

What Nischay is

A career intelligence system that models your child's specific situation — their marks, city, financial constraints, learning style, and long-term probability of satisfaction — before a significant decision is made.

It uses real data. It acknowledges what it doesn't know. It tells your child things they may not want to hear, including when a path is statistically unlikely given their profile. Every estimate includes a confidence level and its source.

What Nischay is not

It is not a system that tells your child to ignore you. It is not a system that optimizes for rebellion. It does not make decisions. It provides information and probability distributions so that decisions — yours and your child's — can be made with better inputs.

Every recommendation shows its data source. Every confidence level shows why. If Nischay doesn't have reliable data, it says so rather than manufacturing confidence.

The goal is not to replace your judgment. The goal is to give your judgment better material to work with.

Nischay AI is a career intelligence tool. It does not replace professional counseling, medical advice, or parental judgment. All probability estimates are data-grounded, uncertainty-aware, and should be weighed alongside human context.
